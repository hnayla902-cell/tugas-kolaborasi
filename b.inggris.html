Waste is leftover material or discarded items produced from a certain production process. In this observation, I will explain about bottle cap waste. Bottle cap waste is trash that comes from bottle caps made of plastic (usually HDPE) weighing around 16 grams.

The body of the bottle cap is the main part, shaped flat and round, usually made of plastics such as polypropylene (polyethylene) or metal (aluminum). The inner thread is the part inside the cap that has a spiral (circular ridged) shape, functioning to lock onto the thread on the bottle neck. The sealing layer (seal) is usually a thin layer of plastic or rubber on the inside, functioning to keep the bottle air-tight so the contents stay fresh.

The safety ring (ring seal) is the lower part of the plastic bottle cap that attaches to the bottle neck. The leftover fragments are small pieces from the ring or thread that often scatter when the cap is opened. Bottle caps can be made into bags to help reduce the amount of plastic waste. Besides that, they can promote a recycling lifestyle. By making bags from bottle caps, we not only help protect the environment but also create products that are functional and aesthetic. This can be done by arranging bottle caps of various colors and sizes and adding tape as the handle.
